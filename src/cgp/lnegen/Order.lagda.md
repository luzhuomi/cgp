### Definition 29 : LNE ordering among parse trees

Let r be a non problematic regular expression

Let vвӮҒ vвӮӮ be parse trees of r.
We define the ordering among v1 and v2 as follows


Note: r вҠў vвӮҒ > vвӮӮ means vвӮҒ is greater than vвӮӮ in LNE ordering. 



### Definition 34: >-Strict increaseing PDInstance*

Let r be a non problematic regular expression.
Let w be a word.
Let pdi be a PDInstance* w.r.t r and w.
We say pdi is >-inc (strict increasing) iff,
  1. p be the partial derivative descendant inhabited in pdi, and
  2. inj is the injection function from parse tress of p to parse tress of r.
  3. for all parse trees p, uвӮҒ and uвӮӮ where p вҠў uвӮҒ > uвӮӮ
  Then r вҠў inj uвӮҒ > inj uвӮӮ

```agda
    { sound-ev : вҲҖ ( x : U p ) вҶ’ (projвӮҒ ( flat {r} (inj x ) ) вүЎ w ++ (projвӮҒ (flat {p} x))) }
```
